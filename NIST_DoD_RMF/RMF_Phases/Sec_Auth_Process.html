Security Authorization Process

The Preparatory Step for the RMF Process

Provides security authorization process and procedures which emphasizes:


  • Building information seuciryt capabilities
  • Maintaining awareness
  • Providing essential information to senior leaders to facilitate decisions

What is authorization?

  • Security authorization is the official management decision given by a senior organizational official to authorize operation of an information system and to explicitly accept the risk to the organizational operations and assets, individuals, other organizations, and the nation based on the implementation of an agreed-upon set of security controls.
    • US Gov't Systems MUST have an authorization to operate in place
