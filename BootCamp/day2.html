Wireless Security

Abbreviations
  • WEP - IV
    • RC4 (40bit) + (24bit)
  • WPA - TKIP
    • RC4 (128bit) + (128bit)
    • 802.11i -- 802.1x > RADIUS (AAA) > enterprise mode > certificate
  • WPA2 - CCMP
    • AES - anything less than 128bit is considered weak
  • WPA3
    • Newest standard - not fully implemented in the wild
    • Implements SAE
